About Us

Meet the Shearkut Team

Shear Kut Barbershop is the proud home to some of South Florida's best barbers. Since 1976, we have been serving the residents of Lake Worth with the best barber services around. Take some time to meet our amazing barbers below, and once you sit in their chairs, you'll become part of the Shear Kut Team as well.

Jeff - Owner

Jeff is the owner and head barber at Shear Kut Barbershop. Jeff was born in Long Beach, California and moved to South Florida at the age of 8 with his mother and two sisters. After graduating highschool in 2001, Jeff worked with his stepfather building pools for 4 years. After 4 years of hard labor 6 days a week Jeff said to himself what many people have in the past, “there has to be another way”. Given the fact that Jeff came from a long line of sylists and barbers in his family, it only made sense that he followed that path and graduated top of his class from the Florida Barber Academy. Barbering became second nature to Jeff and he wanted to grasp a deeper understanding of the trade by collecting and seeking out antique barber tools to perfect his craft. Through his pursuit of antique barber tools he met fellow barber and previous owner of Shear Kut, Franklin Reed. Mr. Reed and Jeff hit it off instantaneously and after joining Shear Kut, Jeff eventually bought the shop to continue its legacy as Downtown Lake Worth’s best barber shop. Jeff has been operating Shear Kut for over 7 years now and has become well known and respected in the industry and community. Jeff has major plans in the works for Shear Kut in the future as it continues to grow.

Miguel

Miguel is a well known barber in Lake Worth Florida for his great attitude and dedication to his craft. At a young age, Miguel began as a professional chef across the US and abroad. After mastering the culinary world, Miguel attended and graduated the Hollywood Institute of Beauty, where he began to master the craft of barbering. Miguel constantly seeks out new trends and challenges. He is a tonsorial master, and is always eager to get his hands on your hair. Miguel is now an important member of the team at Shear Kut Barbershop where he is sometimes known as Miguel Scissor-hands.

Sebastian

Hello my name is Sebastian Sandoval, 26 years old and I’m very passionate about cutting hair. It isn’t just about giving a haircut, its more of the relationship you build with each and every one of your clients. There is always a different story to be told while sitting in my chair and for the most part you’ll be surprised how much in common you have with a person you just met for the first time. The most rewarding feeling is knowing a person feels better about themselves by “just a trim” or even a “hot towel shave”. It’s an honor to be part of a team like the one we have here at Shear Kut Barbershop. Don’t believe me? Stop by and see for yourself… after all the best referrals are by word of mouth.
